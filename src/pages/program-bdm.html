FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience in marketing or technology?
No. The program starts from marketing fundamentals and builds up — it's designed for complete beginners as well as working professionals.
Is this program online or offline?
This program is fully offline, at our Pune campus.
How long is the program?
6 months, structured across progressive learning tracks.
Will I need to buy any tools or software?
Most tools used in the program have free tiers sufficient for learning (Canva, ChatGPT free tier, Google's suite of tools). Where a premium tool is used (e.g., SEMrush), guidance will be provided on affordable access options.
What will I be able to do by the end of the program?
You'll have hands-on experience across SEO, paid ads, social media, AI tools, analytics, and website building — along with a portfolio of real projects you've built during the program.
Is this suitable for someone who wants to start their own business, not get a job?
Yes — the curriculum is built to serve both outcomes: job-readiness and the practical skills to run your own marketing.
